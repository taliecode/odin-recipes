Icecream recipe | Step-by-step

description

Ice cream a creamy, frozen dessert with endless flavors and toppings, perfect for refreshing, sweet indulgence on any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups (480 ml) heavy cream
  • 1 cup (240 ml) whole mil
  • 3/4 cup (150 g) granulated sugar
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Mix the Base:
    • In a large mixing bowl, whisk together heavy cream, whole milk, and sugar until the sugar is fully dissolved.
    • Stir in the vanilla extract.
  2. Chill the Mixture:
    • Cover the mixture and refrigerate for at least 2 hours , or until chilled.
  3. Churn the Ice Cream:
    • Pour the chilled mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions (usually 20-25 minutes).
  4. Freeze and Serve:
    • Transfer the churned ice cream to an airtight container and freeze for at least 2-3 hours to firm up.
    • Scoop and enjoy!

Enjoy!
